Water Facility.

At Nimai Girls Hostel, the management ensures that all residents have access to a continuous supply of clean water.This means that not only is there ample water for drinking . but also for other essential needs such as taking a bath or doing laundry.

Nirmala Chikse's dedication and commitment to running Nimai Girls Hostel has made it a popular choice among women looking for a comfortable and secure stay.
